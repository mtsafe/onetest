Diabetes Coaching Programs and Useful Resources

One Test at a Time Diabetic Coaching offers various affordable coaching services to fit your needs. We will also work around your busy schedule to make sure you receive the coaching time you deserve. Take a look at our services below and select the one that will work for you.

Free Coaching Consultation - $0.00

Schedule your FREE coaching consultation and tell us about your Diabetic journey, learn about your coach's Diabetic journey, and see how working together will help you live your BEST Diabetic life! Consultations are one hour and preferably on Zoom, but we can accommodate your communication needs.

Schedule your FREE coaching consultation by going to our Online Calendar.

Monthly Coaching Program - $200

With the Monthly Coaching program, you will receive:

  • Weekly 60 minute one-on-one coaching sessions
  • Blood sugar and food diary analysis
  • Emergency coaching as needed
  • Weekly meal plans to fit your taste
  • Exercise routines to fit your fitness level
  • Access to the private Facebook group for paid members (coming soon!)
  • Digital Diabetic courses (coming soon!)
  • Access to informational and inspirational webinars
  • Diabetic Supply Assistance as needed
  • Flexible payment plans at $50/week or pay upfront and get $25 off!

Flexible Coaching Program - $50.00

We understand you have a busy life and may need a coaching service that is more flexible and affordable. That is why we offer our Flexible Coaching program for only $50 a session. With the Flexible Coaching Program, you will receive:

  • One 60 minute one-on-one coaching session
  • Blood sugar and food diary analysis
  • One weekly meal plan to fit your taste
  • One weekly exercise routine to fit your fitness level
  • Access to the private Facebook group for paid members (coming soon!)

Free Monthly Coaching Calls

Once a month our Founder and Head Diabetic Coach, Mary Williams, holds FREE Diabetic coaching calls to educate about the different things that can affect your Diabetes. She invites other Diabetics onto her call so they can give their own life experiences to help inspire and to help our audience relate to the frustrations that this relentless disease can cause. Follow us on Facebook for updates on upcoming coaching calls that are absolutely FREE for you to attend.
